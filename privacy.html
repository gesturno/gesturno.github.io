Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

About GesTurno

GesTurno is an Android application designed to help users manage work shifts, working hours, schedules, holidays, notes and calendar planning.

Data Ownership

All data managed through GesTurno belongs exclusively to the user. The developer of GesTurno does not claim ownership of any user data.

Users maintain full control over their information at all times and may edit, export, synchronize or delete their data whenever they choose.

Information We Collect

GesTurno does not collect, store, sell, rent or share personal information on servers controlled by the developer.

Application data is stored locally on the user's device unless the user explicitly enables optional synchronization features.

Google Calendar Integration

GesTurno offers an optional integration with Google Calendar. This integration is only activated when the user explicitly grants permission.

If enabled, GesTurno may access Google Calendar in order to:

  • Create calendar events related to work shifts.
  • Read events required for synchronization.
  • Update existing shift events.
  • Delete synchronized shift events.
  • Create or manage calendars used exclusively for GesTurno synchronization.

Google Calendar data is used exclusively to provide the synchronization functionality requested by the user.

GesTurno does not use Google Calendar data for advertising, marketing, profiling, analytics, artificial intelligence training, or any purpose unrelated to calendar synchronization.

Google Calendar data is never sold, rented, transferred or disclosed to third parties.

GesTurno does not upload Google Calendar data to servers controlled by the developer.

How We Use Data

Any data accessed through Google Calendar is processed solely to perform the actions requested by the user and to maintain synchronization between GesTurno and Google Calendar.

No automated decisions, profiling activities or marketing activities are performed using user data.

Data Storage

Most application data is stored locally on the user's device.

When Google Calendar synchronization is enabled, data is exchanged directly between the user's device and Google's services through authorized APIs.

The developer does not maintain independent copies of user calendar data.

Data Deletion

Users may stop synchronization at any time from within GesTurno.

Users may also revoke GesTurno's access to their Google Account at any time through Google's security settings.

Uninstalling the application removes locally stored application data from the device, subject to the behavior of the Android operating system and any device backup services enabled by the user.

Third-Party Services

GesTurno may use Google services required to provide calendar synchronization. Use of those services is subject to Google's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Compliance with Google API Services User Data Policy

GesTurno's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
