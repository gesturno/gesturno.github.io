Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

About GesTurno

GesTurno is an Android application designed to help users manage work shifts, working hours, notes, holidays, schedules and calendar planning.

Information We Collect

GesTurno does not collect personal data on external servers. Application data is stored locally on the user's device.

Google Calendar Integration

If the user chooses to connect a Google account, GesTurno may access Google Calendar to create, read, update or delete calendar events related to work shifts and schedules.

Calendar information is used exclusively to provide synchronization features requested by the user.

GesTurno does not sell, share or transfer Google user data to third parties.

Data Storage

User data is stored locally on the device. If Google Calendar synchronization is enabled, calendar information is processed only for synchronization purposes.

Data Deletion

Users may stop using synchronization at any time by disconnecting their Google account or uninstalling the application.
